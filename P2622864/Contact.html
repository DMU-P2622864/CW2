About me

I am a puffin, the English call me puffin, but my real family name is AUK which in Icelandic means sea bird. In fact, Iceland is my home country, and my other friends also live in other areas of the Atlantic Ocean. But mostly we all live in Iceland with about 10 million of us. (We are three times as numerous as humans).
I am a pelagic sea bird, which means I enjoy living in the open sea, which is my natural habitat. In fact, I am an excellent swimmer thanks to my short wings, but this cause me that when I fly I need to move my wings rapidly (up to 400 times per min). That's why I fly low over the ocean's surface.
I also like to call myself a clever bird, do you know that I can scratch my back with a stick without hands?
And I can also hold several and with several I want to say a dozen or more fish in my beak, to bring more food to my child.
